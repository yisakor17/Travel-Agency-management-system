Key Features and Benefits

Centralized Ticket & Visa Management

Efficiently track and manage all ticket and visa-related information, including issuance, pricing, void, refund, and reissue statuses. The intuitive interface reduces manual errors and saves valuable time.

Advanced Data Filtering

Find specific information quickly and easily by using various filter parameters available in the filtering panel. This capability saves time and enhances efficiency when searching for customer or ticket details.

Comprehensive Customer Database

Maintain detailed client profiles with contact information, travel preferences, passport details, and loyalty program numbers. This ensures personalized service and quick access to critical data.

Integrated To-Do List

Empower your team with a built-in, customizable to-do list for daily operations. This feature allows for assigning tasks, setting topics, and tracking responsibilities, ensuring no critical item is missed and workflows are optimized.

Financial Tracking

Gain clear insights into your customers' financial ledgers with automated tracking of ticket prices, visas, receipts, and advance payments. This simplifies accounting and provides a real-time overview of your finances.

Secure Document Management

Safely store and access important documents like ticket PDFs and passport images, linked directly to customer and ticket records. The system ensures easy retrieval and secure handling of sensitive information.

Offline Capability

Designed with reliability in mind, the platform's core functionalities can operate efficiently even without a constant internet connection, ensuring business continuity in diverse operational environments.

Generate Excel Reports

Easily export your data to an Excel spreadsheet to gain a clearer insight into your ticketing sales, commissions, and more. This provides powerful data analysis capabilities for business growth.
